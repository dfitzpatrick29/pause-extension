      Quick Start

      Use Pause to make browsing intentional:

      • Add distracting domains in Blocked Sites (you can remove them anytime).
      • When you open a blocked site, write a short reason before continuing.
      • Use Journal to review your past reasons and patterns.
      • Set a check-in time in Timer to be prompted again later.
      • All settings are saved in your browser and stay until you clear them.
